Scatterplot Chart for Covid

This graph shows the number of new deaths that were decreased from the new cases of covid due to the deliverable of vaccinations and adding restrictions specically for Covid-19. When hovering over this scatterplot we see the maximum number of deaths that were decreased from the new Covid cases that were recently occured. Although some deaths may still occur from Covid at least the graph proves there is still a chance to not only decrease but also to prevent deaths from Covid due to vaccines as well as people following restrictions although the chances are slim the main importance are for people to follow safety rules and avoid covid.
